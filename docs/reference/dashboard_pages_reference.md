# Dashboard Pages Reference

## Executive Insurance Overview
Audience: executives. KPIs: active policies, premium revenue, total claims, claims ratio, fraud risk rate.

## Claims Operations
Audience: claims team. KPIs: open claims, approved claims, rejected claims, average claim amount, processing time.

## Policy Portfolio
Audience: product managers. KPIs: policies by type, policy status, premium revenue, sales channel performance.

## Fraud Risk Monitoring
Audience: fraud analysts. KPIs: high-risk claims, average risk score, suspicious amount flags, duplicate flags, late report flags.

## Agent and Regional Performance
Audience: sales and regional managers. KPIs: policies by agent, premium by agent, claims ratio by agent, regional performance.

## Data Quality Monitoring
Audience: data engineers and compliance. KPIs: quarantine counts, invalid reasons, data quality pass rate.
