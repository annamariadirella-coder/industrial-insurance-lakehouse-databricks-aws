# Day 2 Orientation

Day 2 is the trust-building day of the industrial insurance lakehouse.

On Day 1, you created raw files in S3 and Bronze Delta tables.

Bronze answers:

> What arrived?

Day 2 asks:

> What can be trusted and reused?

In a real German insurance company, data arrives from policy systems, customer systems, claims systems, payment systems, broker systems, and fraud systems.

This data can contain duplicates, invalid values, broken relationships, and GDPR-sensitive personal fields.

Your job is to transform Bronze into trusted Silver.
