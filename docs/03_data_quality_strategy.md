# Day 2 Data Quality Strategy

Data quality is critical in insurance.

Wrong insurance data can cause wrong financial reporting, incorrect fraud analysis, bad customer analytics, and compliance risk.

Your Day 2 strategy should include:

1. Primary key validation
2. Foreign key validation
3. Business status validation
4. Amount validation
5. Date validation
6. Duplicate handling
7. Quarantine of invalid records
8. Row-count comparison
9. Documentation of results

Silver is not complete when the notebook runs.  
Silver is complete when quality results are visible and explainable.
